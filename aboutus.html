About SMARTCONVO

Learn more about SMARTCONVO and how the platform enhances the convocation experience through smart technology integration.

Introduction to SMARTCONVO
SMARTCONVO is an AI-Assisted Convocation Queue and Identity Verification System developed for TVET MARA / IKM Lumut. The platform is designed to modernize convocation management by combining real-time queue tracking, smart identity verification, and interactive digital services into one centralized system.
Purpose of the System
SMARTCONVO helps improve the overall efficiency and organization of convocation ceremonies while providing a smoother experience for students, guests, and administrators.
